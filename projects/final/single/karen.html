Taste of Home

by Karen Li

"Hot pot is a fun communal dining experience where people cook food together in a boiling pot. Hot pot offers a range of options and is very customizable. You can choose to have a spicy soup base if you prefer, and you can cook almost anything - from beef to vegetables to noodles to tofu."
