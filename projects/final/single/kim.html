Breathe

by Kim Quach

Breathe is an animation adapted from a poem Kim wrote depicting the feelings of anxiety.

Content warning: "this piece deals with my feelings of anxiety and depression, and there are flashing lights in this piece. This animation may be ill-suited for those sensitive to the above material."

Piano composition by Mateo Mok, Voice track help by Joseph Cho
